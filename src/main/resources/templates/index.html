Welcome to Code Fellowship

Engaging in a coding bootcamp is not for the faint of heart.
Committing to six months of intensive training will push you to your limits.
In order to complete this journey, you'll need to wield every tool in your
arsenal, including the people around you.

This web app exists to connect fellow bootcamp trainees in their path
through code fellows and beyond. Sign up here to connect, share resources,
and commiserate. Hoo-ah!
